# 🚀 Data Science & Analytics Internship – Task 1
## Sales & Profit Insights Dashboard

### 📌 About the Task
In this real-world internship task, the objective was to analyze business sales data and answer key strategic business questions.

This project focuses on helping businesses understand:

- Which products generate the most revenue?
- How sales change over time?
- Which categories and regions are most profitable?
- Where the business should focus to grow faster?

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

This interactive Power BI dashboard provides an executive-level summary of:

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Profit Margin
- Monthly Sales Trends
- Category & Sub-Category Performance
- Regional Sales Distribution

---

## 🔍 Key KPIs

- Total Sales: 2.30M
- Total Profit: 286.40K
- Total Orders: 5,010
- Profit Margin: 12%

---

## 📈 Insights Generated

✔ Technology category contributes highest revenue  
✔ Certain sub-categories drive majority of profit  
✔ Sales show seasonal growth pattern  
✔ California & New York are top-performing states
